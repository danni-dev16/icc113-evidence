How unemployment weakens communities, skills, and future markets

South Africa's unemployment crisis is not an isolated labour issue. It weakens connected systems such as income, health, education, equality, and economic growth. This evidence pack explains how unemployment creates a domino effect that affects both communities and future business markets.

01 — The Problem

Unequal Access
Starts the Collapse

Jobs, infrastructure, education, and transport remain unevenly distributed across South Africa. Rural and township communities often face fewer pathways into employment, limiting economic participation and increasing vulnerability.

⚠️

What Is Wrong

Unemployment remains high and persistent, entrenched across multiple generations and regions.

👥

Who Is Affected

Rural communities, township residents, youth, and low-income households bear the greatest burden.

📉

Why It Matters

Reduced income weakens consumer spending, limits skills development, and shrinks the size of future markets.

🗺️

Spatial Inequality

Location, infrastructure, and transport determine access. Those without these are structurally excluded.

"Unemployment is the first domino in a wider system collapse."

02 — The Domino Effect

The Domino Effect Across
Connected Systems

The collapse is not only linear. Each weakened system intensifies others. Click each domino to expand.

💼
01
Unemployment
Loss of work reduces household income and blocks participation in the economy. It is the entry point through which all other failures cascade.
📊
02
Poverty
Without stable income, households struggle to meet basic needs — food, shelter, transport, and education become unaffordable.
🌾
03
Food Insecurity
Reduced income limits access to reliable nutrition, affecting physical development, cognitive performance, and workforce readiness.
🏥
04
Poor Health
Poverty and food insecurity worsen both physical and mental health outcomes, increasing the burden on public health systems and reducing productivity.
📚
05
Skills Loss
Long-term unemployment reduces professional experience, training opportunities, and overall employability — locking communities out of economic recovery.
⚖️
06
Inequality
Communities with fewer resources, skills, and infrastructure fall further behind, widening the gap between advantaged and disadvantaged populations.
📉
07
Shrinking Market
Lower income and weaker workforce readiness reduce consumer spending and business growth, ultimately shrinking the market that businesses depend on.

"Each weakened system places pressure on the others — creating wider systemic collapse."

05 — Investigation Method

How We Investigated
the Problem

The investigation used labour-market data, poverty trends, inequality indicators, and spatial analysis to trace how unemployment spreads through connected systems.

📋

Labour Data

Examined unemployment, youth unemployment, and expanded unemployment trends from official quarterly releases.

📐

Poverty & Inequality Indicators

Used poverty headcount ratios and Gini coefficient data to understand how unemployment affects household vulnerability.

📈

Trend Analysis

Assessed whether unemployment is a temporary shock or a structural, persistent condition — with clear findings of the latter.

🗺️

Spatial / GIS Thinking

Identified how location, transport access, and infrastructure shape who can access economic opportunity and who cannot.

🌍

SDG Systems Analysis

Connected unemployment to multiple Sustainable Development Goals to show the full breadth of the crisis.

06 — Business Relevance

Why Business Owners
Should Care

When unemployment weakens communities,
businesses lose both workers and customers.
🛒

Smaller Customer Base

When household income falls, consumer spending drops. Fewer employed people means fewer buyers for your products and services.

🔧

Weaker Workforce Pipeline

Long-term unemployment reduces skills, experience, and job readiness. Hiring from a depleted talent pool becomes harder and more costly.

📊

Slower Business Growth

Weak demand discourages expansion, hiring, and capital investment. Economic stagnation becomes self-reinforcing.

💼

Lower Investor Confidence

Unstable labour markets and high inequality create uncertainty, reducing the attractiveness of the business environment.

🏘️

Higher Social Pressure

Poverty, crime risk, and overburdened public services create an operating environment that is harder and more expensive to navigate.

"A collapsing social system becomes a shrinking business market."

07 — Opportunity

Strengthening the System
Before It Falls

Addressing unemployment can strengthen multiple systems at once. Investment is not charity — it is strategic market-building.

🏗️

Infrastructure Investment

Improves access to jobs, services, and business activity — particularly in underserved rural and township areas.

🎓

Skills Development

Builds a stronger workforce pipeline, improving both employability and the quality of the talent market.

⚙️

Ethical Engineering

Ensures projects are sustainable, transparent, and genuinely beneficial to communities — not extractive.

🤝

Local Economic Participation

More employed people means more income, more consumer spending, and stronger local markets.

📈

Long-Term Market Stability

Stronger communities produce more resilient businesses. Employment investment grows markets.

Fixing unemployment does not just create jobs —
it creates customers, strengthens demand,
and builds future markets.

12 — Get Your QR Code

How to Publish This Site
& Generate a QR Code

To link your poster QR code to this website, you need to host it online. Here are the three fastest free options — each takes under 5 minutes.

⭐

Option 1 — Netlify Drop (Recommended · Free)

The easiest option. No account required to start.

  1. Go to netlify.com/drop
  2. Put index.html and your PDF files in one folder
  3. Drag that entire folder onto the Netlify page
  4. Netlify gives you a live URL instantly — e.g. https://abc123.netlify.app
  5. Paste that URL into a free QR generator (see below)
🐙

Option 2 — GitHub Pages (Free · Permanent)

Best if you want a permanent, named URL.

  1. Create a free account at github.com
  2. Create a new repository named icc113-evidence
  3. Upload index.html + PDF files
  4. Go to Settings → Pages → set source to main branch
  5. Your URL: https://yourusername.github.io/icc113-evidence
🔺

Option 3 — Vercel (Free · Fast)

Slightly more setup, very fast globally.

  1. Go to vercel.com and sign in with GitHub
  2. Click New Project → drag in your folder
  3. Deploy — Vercel generates a live URL instantly
  4. Use that URL for your QR code

Once you have your live URL → go to qr.io or qrcode-monkey.com to generate a styled QR code. Download it as SVG or PNG and place it on your poster.
